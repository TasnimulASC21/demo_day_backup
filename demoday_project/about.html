HOW WE STARTED:

KOROSENSEI WAS STARTED BY JAMEER FREDRICK, TASNIMUL ISLAM, VIDHUR BUSANNAGARI, AND WILLIAM TSAKU WITH THE GOAL OF GIVING PEOPLE

WHO DO NOT HAVE ACCESS TO EDUCATION AN OPPERTUNITY .

ABOUT KOROSENSEI:

KOROSENSEI IS A PEER TO PEER TUTORING WEBSITE WHICH PROVIDES A LIST OF QUALIFIED PROFESSIONALS WHO WILL HELP PEOPLE WITH WHATEVER

SUBJECT THEY NEED HELP WITH WHICH WILL INCLUDE EACH PERSON'S EMAIL AND PHONE NUMBER BEING DISPLAYED ON THE CONTACT PAGE.
